Job Title: Digital Marketing & Sales Specialist

Job Description:
We are looking for a dynamic Digital Marketing and Sales Specialist to drive our growth. The ideal candidate will have strong experience in sales strategies, lead generation, and digital marketing campaigns.

Responsibilities:
- Plan and execute digital marketing strategies, including SEO/SEM, email, social media, and display advertising.
- Drive sales growth through lead generation and conversion optimization.
- Analyze market trends and customer insights to optimize marketing spend.
- Collaborate with the sales team to align marketing efforts with sales goals.
- Measure and report performance of all digital marketing campaigns.

Requirements:
- Proven working experience in digital marketing or sales.
- Demonstrable experience leading and managing SEO/SEM, marketing database, email, social media and/or display advertising campaigns.
- Solid knowledge of website analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics).
- Strong analytical skills and data-driven thinking.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.

Skills Required:
- Marketing
- Sales
- SEO
- Social Media Marketing
- Lead Generation
- Communication
- Analytics
